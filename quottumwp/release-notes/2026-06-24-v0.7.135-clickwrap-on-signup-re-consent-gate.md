## What's new

### Clickwrap on signup
Both **Create account** and the **beta-signup waitlist** form now require an explicit checkbox to accept the **[Terms of Service](https://quottum.io/terms)** and acknowledge the **[Privacy Policy](https://quottum.io/privacy)**. The submit button is disabled until checked.

### Acceptance log
Every acceptance is recorded with the user, document type (ToS / Privacy / Beta), the version they saw, server-captured IP + user-agent, and the document URL — so we can later prove which exact wording each user agreed to.

### Re-consent on material change
The first time you sign in after we publish a material ToS or Privacy update, a blocking modal asks you to review and accept the new version before continuing. Operators impersonating a workspace skip the gate (the workspace owner is the legal party, not the operator).

A `TOS_VERSION` constant is the single knob — bumping it on the next material change re-prompts everyone on next dashboard load.
