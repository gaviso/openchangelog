## Fixes & improvements
- **Changing your password** now asks for your **current password** (required by our account-security settings) — fixing password changes from the new Account page.
- For sign-ins older than 24 hours, a password change now confirms with a **one-time code emailed to you** before it applies.
- Clearer email-change status: a pending change reminds you to confirm the links sent to **both** your current and new inbox.
