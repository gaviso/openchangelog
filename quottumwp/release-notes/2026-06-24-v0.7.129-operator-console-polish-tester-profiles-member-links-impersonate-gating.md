## Operator console polish
- Beta tester rows now show profile detail at a glance — workspace + status, joined date, feedback count, granted-features ratio, and the signup intake answers. Click any tester to open their full profile.
- Workspace member rows are clickable too — jump straight to the user's profile from any workspace detail.
- Impersonate button is disabled (with a clear tooltip) when the workspace hasn't enabled Support access, instead of clicking → erroring.
- Cleaned up a dead "Back to dashboard" link in the operator sidebar (operators don't have a workspace to go to).
