Pending invitations row gets a **Resend** button. Stale \"email delivery lands in v0.6.0\" copy from the panel description is gone too.

### What you'll see

Settings → Permissions → Pending invitations row now has three buttons:

\`\`\`
[📋 Copy link]  [📤 Resend]  [✕ Revoke]
\`\`\`

**Resend behavior:**

- Click → spinner → ✓ \"Resent\" for 2s → back to default
- Re-fires the same branded \`workspace.invite\` email to the same recipient
- **Bumps \`expires_at\` to 14 days from now** — resets the clock so the recipient gets a fresh window without you needing to revoke + recreate
- 404'd if the invitation doesn't belong to your workspace or was already accepted

Common case it solves: invite landed in spam, recipient lost it, or the 14-day window is about to lapse.

### Refactor along the way

Extracted the email-fire block from \`POST /api/workspaces/invitations\` into \`src/lib/workspace-invites.ts → sendWorkspaceInviteEmail()\`. Both create AND resend share that one path now, so variable shape (\`workspace_name\`, \`invited_by_name\`, \`role\`, \`invite_link\`, \`recipient_email\`), link host, and inviter-name lookup can never drift between them.

### Copy cleanup

The Invite-a-teammate panel still claimed \"email delivery lands in v0.6.0 — for now copy the link manually.\" Email delivery shipped in v0.6.5 and resend shipped here. Updated to:

> We'll email them a branded sign-in link. They have 14 days to accept; you can resend or revoke from the list below.
